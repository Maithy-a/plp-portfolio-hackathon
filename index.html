About

I'm Bonface Maithya, a design engineer based in Kenya.

What I actually do:
I fix broken interfaces — not just visually, but functionally too. I design with code and code with design in mind. While most designers can't code and most developers avoid design, I live at the intersection of both.

While others debate aesthetics, I'm delivering pixel-perfect, high-performing UIs. ARIA compliance? That’s not a nice-to-have — it’s a standard I live by.

I thrive on solving complex UI challenges, beating tight deadlines, and exceeding expectations. I believe the best work happens through collaboration, and I'm always ready to bring ideas to life — beautifully and effectively.

When I’m not building interfaces, you’ll find me painting, catching up on anime, or getting lost in the world of F1 and football.
