My way of getting things done!

My process is clear and intentional rooted in the waterfall methodology, guiding each phase from discovery to deployment. In just 4 weeks, I'll deliver a purposeful, impactful website that aligns with your vision and elevates your brand.

This is where everything begins. You fill out a detailed brief to outline your goals, preferences, and expectations. The more specific, the better — it sets the tone for everything that follows.

I dive deep into understanding your industry, competitors, and audience. This includes visual analysis, UX audits, and market research to identify what works — and how we can do it better.

Based on the insights gathered, I create interactive wireframes or low-fidelity prototypes. This step maps out user flows and page structures — ensuring functionality and user intent align before design begins.

This is where the brand comes alive. Using your guidelines and creative direction, I design the final UI — polished layouts, typography, color palettes, and interactions that bring your vision to life.

Once everything is tested and approved, your website goes live. I provide all source files, documentation, and optional training to ensure a smooth handoff and easy future updates.
